A WORLD WITHOUT HOLIDAYS

THE TRILOGY: HARMORIZATION, PASSING, AND DISTANT LANDS.

 DESIGN MULTIMEDIA

The A World without Holidays trilogy is about a group of students (the NOVICE) that struggle to fight the evil faction (the MENTORS), who intend to cancel all holidays. The first part was shot in 2009, while the second and third part are rendered using World of Warcraft graphics engine in 2011 and 2012. Due to some complicated reasons, only the third part is released.
